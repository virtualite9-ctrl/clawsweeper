## Summary

Keep this issue open. Current main still has the Kimi thinking/subagent failure shape: Kimi can send thinking-enabled requests, but its provider wrapper and replay policy do not preserve or fill `reasoning_content` for replayed assistant tool-call messages. The broader thinking-block fixes and the DeepSeek/Venice placeholder fixes do not cover Kimi.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and fix this in the Kimi provider or a documented generic provider-compat seam. The likely implementation is a Kimi/Moonshot replay payload wrapper that preserves existing `reasoning_content` or fills a provider-accepted placeholder on assistant tool-call replay when Kimi thinking is enabled, with focused regression coverage for `sessions_spawn` and tool-call continuation under Kimi thinking. If maintainers choose a shared path, align it with the still-open #50178 provider-required `reasoning_content` replay gap.
