## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the requested `feishu_bitable_delete_record` tool. In `extensions/feishu/src/bitable.ts`, the public Bitable schemas and registrations cover get/list/create/update/create-field flows only; there is no delete-record schema or registered tool. `main` does call `client.bitable.appTableRecord.delete` internally during placeholder-row cleanup in app creation, but that is not the requested user-facing tool.
