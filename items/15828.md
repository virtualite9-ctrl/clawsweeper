## Summary

Keep #15828 open. Current main still only saves bundled `session-memory` on `/new` and `/reset`; idle/daily session rollover emits typed plugin lifecycle hooks but does not invoke the internal bundled hook. Shutdown saves, message-count/periodic checkpoints, configurable trigger lists, and LLM-generated summary output are still not implemented. The same-day maintainer/Codex review in the issue also explicitly kept this open, and the related PRs #61675 and #71792 remain only narrower proposed fixes for the idle/daily subset.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #15828 open unless maintainers intentionally split it. The product path is to extend bundled `session-memory` with explicit configurable idle, shutdown, command, and message-count/periodic triggers; define dedupe and summary-generation behavior; wire the chosen lifecycle path through source, config schema/docs, and focused tests; and treat #61675/#71792 as narrower idle/daily candidates rather than complete replacements.
