## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `c070509b7f8fdbe479e5d829643782c272e30e83` still has the unguarded `readLoggingConfig()` path in `resolveConsoleSettings()`, and the PR discussion identifies a concrete remaining recursion route plus a safer `forward()` fallback that is not present on `main`. This PR still appears materially useful rather than obsolete.

## Evidence
