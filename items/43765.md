## Risks / Open Questions

- The PR is XL and mixes heartbeat, Feishu channel health, exec/process semantics, ACPX dependency and launch behavior, ACP spawn metadata, diagnostics, and docs, making conservative review and rollback hard.
- The PR branch has concrete module-load blockers reported in review comments: duplicate Feishu logger declarations and a missing session-generation module.
- The ACPX portion adds executable third-party packages and changes process launch behavior, which is a supply-chain and command-execution surface that should be isolated for maintainer/security review.
- Changing all non-zero foreground exec exits from completed to failed is a user-visible agent/tool semantics change and may break workflows that intentionally use non-zero exit codes.
