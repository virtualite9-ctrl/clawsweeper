## Summary

Keep open. Current main has meaningful fixes and mitigations for #38844, including dispatcher-timeout error preservation and an atomic file-chooser route when ref/inputRef/element is supplied, but the specific maintainer-cleanup bar for closing is not met: the public docs/agent guidance still describe upload as a separate arming step before the triggering click, and the related PR discussion itself says selector/batch only reduce the stale-ref workflow rather than directly fixing the chooser flow.
