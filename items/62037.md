## Summary

Current `main` already prevents the machine-user fallback for `systemctl --user` permission-denied failures, and it has a focused regression test for that path. The PR’s specific classifier change is no longer needed and the April 6 review comments correctly identified correctness issues in the proposed patch.

## Evidence

- **Main already skips machine-scope fallback on permission denied:** `shouldFallbackToMachineUserScope()` returns false for permission-denied user-bus failures, and `execSystemctlUser()` stops after the direct `systemctl --user` attempt instead of retrying `--machine <user>@ --user`.
