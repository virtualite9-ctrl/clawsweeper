## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does have generic per-agent `agents.list[].params` overrides, and those overrides are documented, typed, validated, and merged at runtime. But the generic extra-param wrapper only demonstrably forwards `temperature` and `maxTokens` (plus transport/cache-related knobs), while this issue asks for broader per-agent generation controls including `top_p`, `top_k`, and repetition penalties. That makes the request only partially satisfied on current `main`.
