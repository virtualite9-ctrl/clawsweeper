## Summary

Keep open. Current main has meaningful Android chat work, including a Chat tab, assistant entrypoints, Tailscale/LAN connection guidance, and image attachment/rendering support, but this does not fully close the issue’s broader request for maintainer discussion of a chat-first official Android surface and selective UX/media upstreaming. The issue is not stale enough for stale-insufficient-info, is not maintainer/protected, and does not fit a conservative ClawHub close because official Android companion app UX is a repo-owned product surface.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open for maintainer product triage. The best path is to ask the reporter to split the discussion into one or two concrete Android proposals or PR slices, likely around launch-to-chat behavior, connection/onboarding friction, and specific media receive/render/fullscreen gaps, while treating AndroidForClaw and clawchat2 as prior-art references rather than proof that OpenClaw can close this item now.
