## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still shows both reported defects: `runMessageAction` unconditionally carries `input.defaultAccountId` into the target channel path before any cross-provider handling, and the gateway approvals protocol still rejects the `source` field even though durable `allow-always` persistence writes that field. I did not find equivalent coverage or a corresponding mainline fix in the inspected files/history.
