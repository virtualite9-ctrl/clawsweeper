## Summary

Current `main` already routes `config.get` through `redactConfigSnapshot`, and that redaction now explicitly scrubs the `sourceConfig` and `runtimeConfig` alias fields that this issue reported. Regression tests cover those aliases, and the changelog says the fix shipped in `v2026.4.14`.

## Evidence
