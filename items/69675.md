## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still documents and implements direct routing of bound ACP follow-ups to the child session, while this PR proposes a semantics clarification and explicitly says the deeper runtime change is still future work. There is no high-confidence basis to close it as already implemented, unreproducible, or stale.
