## Summary

Keep open: current `main` still lacks both the bundled `outbound-whitelist` plugin and the WhatsApp/Discord direct-delivery `message_sending` hook wiring, and the proposal fits the existing OpenClaw plugin-hook architecture rather than a ClawHub-only scope.
