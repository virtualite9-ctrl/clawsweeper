## Summary

Keep open. Current main still restores the task registry directly from the SQLite store and only logs restore failures; it does not snapshot, rebuild, recover, or replace a malformed `tasks/runs.sqlite`. The issue is new, has concrete error details, has no protected-label or maintainer-author auto-close constraint, and no related duplicate was provided.
