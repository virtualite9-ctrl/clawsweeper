## Summary

Current `main` still accepts `--force-document` at the CLI/action layer, but the gateway send payload/schema does not carry `forceDocument`, and the WhatsApp outbound adapter has no `forceDocument` plumbing. The reported WhatsApp transport gap remains on `main`, so this item should stay open.

## Evidence
