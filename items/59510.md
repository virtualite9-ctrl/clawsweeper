## Summary

Current `main` already implements the requested exec-approval simplifications: a one-step `exec-policy` CLI to sync `tools.exec.*` with host approvals, direct `approvals allowlist add|remove` helpers with wildcard agent support, JSON5-based approvals import via CLI, host-default no-prompt behavior unless tightened, and documented session-only no-prompt shortcuts.

## Evidence

- **One-step local policy sync and YOLO preset:** `openclaw exec-policy` exists specifically to keep `tools.exec.*` and the local `~/.openclaw/exec-approvals.json` aligned in one step, with a `yolo` preset that sets `host=gateway`, `security=full`, `ask=off`, and `askFallback=full`.
