## Summary

Keep #6615 open. The issue asks for first-class exec-approvals denylist behavior, including `security: "denylist"`, `ask: "on-match"`, denylist rule storage, and argument-aware deny matching. Current main still exposes only deny/allowlist/full security, off/on-miss/always ask modes, allowlist-only approvals storage, and allowlist-miss approval logic. The linked SafeClaw and CRE/claude-rule-enforcer work are useful prior art, but they do not ship the requested OpenClaw core exec-approvals surface.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #6615 open until OpenClaw either implements first-class exec-approval denylist support across config/types/schema, approvals normalization and storage, gateway/node/system.run policy, CLI/Control UI, docs, and tests, or maintainers explicitly redirect the feature to ClawHub/plugin policy and track any missing authorization hooks separately.
