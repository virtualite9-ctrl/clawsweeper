## Summary

Current `main` already resolves heartbeat runs to the effective agent, forwards that agent’s `agents.list[].model.fallbacks` as `fallbacksOverride`, and `runWithModelFallback` prefers that override over `agents.defaults.model.fallbacks`. The same implementation is present in the shipped `v2026.4.23` tree.

## Evidence
