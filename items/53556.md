## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already preserves two of the reported Feishu compatibility paths (`groupPolicy: "allowall"` and deprecated `topicSessionMode`), but it does not appear to support the reported `botId -> appId` rename, and I found no Feishu-specific doctor/runtime migration rules that would rewrite or warn on that legacy key.
