## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has some ACP abort/timeout handling, but it does not include the PR's dispatch-layer timeout wrapper, abort-aware delivery suppression, combined-abort hook, or signal-aware reconcile/cleanup changes. The PR is therefore not redundant on `main`, and the review discussion still shows substantive unresolved review points rather than a stale/no-op branch.
