## Summary

Current `main` already has the reasoning-leak fix: reply delivery now prefers `final_answer` text, suppresses commentary-phase output, and strips/promotes `<think>`-style reasoning so it does not reach user-visible replies. The changelog says this shipped in `v2026.4.5`.
