## Summary

Keep open. On current `main` (`cb2c36b04975e34ab7014a11575d6589aa96ae32`), `web_fetch` still does not expose or thread a private-network opt-in comparable to this PR; the public config, runtime schema, docs/help, and tests only cover the narrower `tools.web.fetch.ssrfPolicy.allowRfc2544BenchmarkRange` override.
