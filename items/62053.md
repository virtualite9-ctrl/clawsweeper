## Summary

Current `main` no longer uses the lazy Mattermost proxy that triggered `Reflect.ownKeys` on a non-object, and the direct-export fix was already shipped in `v2026.4.7`.

## Evidence

- **Current main removes the lazy proxy path:** `extensions/mattermost/channel-plugin-api.ts` on current `main` is now only two direct re-exports: `mattermostPlugin` from `channel-plugin-runtime.js` and `mattermostSetupPlugin` from `src/channel.setup.js`. The crashing `Proxy` traps are absent.
