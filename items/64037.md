## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not expose inbound media on `before_agent_reply` hook context, while the inbound pipeline already normalizes that media metadata upstream. The PR remains additive and relevant, and the provided review discussion shows the author addressed the initial alignment and URL-only-media concerns in the PR head.
