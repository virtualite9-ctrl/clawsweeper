## Summary

#63410 is superseded by newer open WhatsApp stability trackers. #66917 carries the same 15-20 minute 408/499 reconnect pattern and dual-account context, while #66920 tracks the concrete remaining user-visible failure after repeated reconnects: group inbound silently stopping while DMs/outbound survive. Current main has generic reconnect, status, watchdog, and reconnect-drain plumbing, but the remaining live stability/keepalive-tuning work should stay consolidated in those newer reports rather than split across this older issue.

## Best Possible Solution

Close #63410 as superseded. Use #66917 as the umbrella for WhatsApp 408/499 stability, keepAliveIntervalMs exposure, and watchdog work, and use #66920 for the narrower group-inbound degradation after reconnect cycles. Any eventual code change likely belongs in `extensions/whatsapp/src/connection-controller.ts`, `extensions/whatsapp/src/reconnect.ts`, or the WhatsApp config/docs surface, with regression coverage around reconnect and group inbound behavior.
