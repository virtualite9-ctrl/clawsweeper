## Summary

Current `main` suppresses commentary-phase partials before channel delivery, strips `[[reply_to_current]]`/other reply directives from visible outbound text, and synthesizes a clean timeout error when a timed-out embedded run has no safe assistant payload. The timeout/abort remainder called out after #61282 appears covered on `main` and shipped in `v2026.4.7`.
