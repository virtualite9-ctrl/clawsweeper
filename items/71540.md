## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has a real mismatch in the security-audit path: phantom-entry detection only exempts installed plugins plus `listChannelPlugins()`, so bundled non-channel plugins such as `anthropic`, `ollama`, `xai`, `brave`, `memory-core`, `memory-wiki`, and `active-memory` can still be reported as phantom even though they are bundled manifests. The issue body overstates one point — bundled plugins are not universally exempt from `plugins.allow` when a restrictive allowlist is in use — but the underlying report remains actionable and is not safely closable.
