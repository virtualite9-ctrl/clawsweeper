## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements `agents.defaults.subagents.runTimeoutSeconds` as a fallback default only, with explicit `sessions_spawn.runTimeoutSeconds` taking precedence and no `minRunTimeoutSeconds` or lock-style config present.
