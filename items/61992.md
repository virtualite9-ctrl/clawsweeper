## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes the typing-indicator TTL at 2 minutes, and the public config/docs only expose `typingMode` and `typingIntervalSeconds`, not a user-configurable timeout. The issue discussion also adds current cross-channel evidence from Discord and Feishu that the fixed 2-minute cutoff is still causing user-visible confusion on long-running runs.
