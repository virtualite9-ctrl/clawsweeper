## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-codes `openrouter/auto` during OpenRouter onboarding, does not expose `free` in the bundled OpenRouter catalog, and still only special-cases `auto` in the shared OpenRouter thinking wrapper. The PR addresses a live gap rather than an already-shipped fix.
