## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-codes a 1s wake-layer retry after `requests-in-flight` heartbeat skips, and the scheduler/tests explicitly preserve that behavior. I found no config/docs surface for a heartbeat retry delay override, so the reported regression remains plausible and unaddressed on current `main`.
