## Risks / Open Questions

- The stale PR predates current `buildUserAgent()` / `User-Agent` handling in `graph-upload.ts`; rebasing must preserve those headers where appropriate.
- The reviewed patch has a known stall-count off-by-one: `UPLOAD_SESSION_MAX_STALLS = 5` with a strict `>` guard allows 6 stalled responses.
- The 4 MiB fallback threshold should be confirmed by the Teams maintainer against the observed failure mode and Microsoft Graph guidance before merge.
- The new chunk PUTs use a Graph-returned preauthenticated `uploadUrl`; that is expected for Graph upload sessions, but review should ensure no user-controlled URL is accepted into that path.
