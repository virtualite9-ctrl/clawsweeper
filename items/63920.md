## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still exposes Slack block actions and modal lifecycle events, but not Slack shortcut or message-action events. The requested surface does not appear implemented in code, tests, or docs.

## Evidence

- **Interaction registration only wires block actions and modal events:** `registerSlackInteractionEvents` calls `registerSlackBlockActionHandler`, then registers `ctx.app.view` and optional `viewClosed`. There is no `ctx.app.shortcut` registration path.
