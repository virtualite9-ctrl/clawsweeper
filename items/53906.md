## Summary

Current main already removes the reported cooldown cascade: transient auth cooldowns are capped and reset after expiry, cooldowns can stay model-scoped for rate limits, and the fallback loop now keeps moving to other candidates/providers instead of stalling after a cooldown probe.

## Evidence

## Risks / Open Questions

- I did not find an explicit cooldown-reset command; cooldown persistence across restart may still be unchanged.
- The implementation is not provider-window-aware in the way the issue proposes; it fixes the outage by capping/resetting transient cooldowns rather than aligning to Anthropic's hourly window.
