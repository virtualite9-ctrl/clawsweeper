## Summary

Current `main` does not show the requested `/agent/...` remap in the read-tool workspace guard. The guard still only rewrites `containerWorkdir` (typically `/workspace`), while `workspaceAccess: "ro"` still mounts the agent workspace at `/agent` and advertises that path to the model. This does not meet the bar for closing as fixed or obsolete.
