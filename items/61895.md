## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still relies on ACPX session-store reset/reconnect behavior for persistent-session repair and does not add OpenClaw-side process-tree cleanup. The bundled `acpx@0.5.3` still respawns when the stored PID is dead, stores only a single child PID, and still falls back to `agent.unref()` when close does not complete. That is too close to the reported orphan-process leak to close conservatively.
