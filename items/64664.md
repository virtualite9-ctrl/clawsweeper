## Summary

Current main still has the reported stale-approval UX problem. Approval-not-found errors are surfaced as the raw `unknown or expired approval id` text in `/approve`, Telegram callback failures are rethrown without stale/restart guidance, and the related narrow fix PR was closed unmerged.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open. The best narrow fix is to preserve current approval invalidation semantics but translate `isApprovalNotFoundError(...)` at the `/approve` and Telegram callback user-facing surfaces into clear guidance that the approval is no longer available, may have expired or been invalidated by a gateway restart, and should be re-requested. Add focused regression coverage in `src/auto-reply/reply/commands-approve.test.ts` and the Telegram callback tests. Durable approval persistence can remain a separate product decision.
