## Summary

Current main, and the shipped v2026.4.23 tree, already cover the reported Telegram DM/Groq audio regression: DM voice-note preflight is restored, multipart Groq/OpenAI-compatible audio uploads are built and normalized correctly, and bundled media providers referenced from `tools.media.audio` are resolved through compat loading.
