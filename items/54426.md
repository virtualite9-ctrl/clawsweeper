## Summary

Close #54426 as superseded by the broader trusted-proxy/service-identity track in #69066. The PR targets real ACP/trusted-proxy pain, but its blanket localDirect trusted-proxy bypass conflicts with current main's fail-closed loopback contract, regression tests, docs, and the P1 review warning about colocated reverse-proxy bypass risk.

## Best Possible Solution

Close this PR as superseded by #69066. Keep current main's fail-closed trusted-proxy loopback behavior and pursue a first-class internal service identity, or another explicitly gated fallback reviewed under the canonical trusted-proxy auth track, rather than merging this blanket localDirect method:none bypass.
