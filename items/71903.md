## Summary

Keep open. PR #71903 is a focused, recent bug-fix PR and current main does not contain its core behavior change: `shouldTreatAsOrphanSelfLock` still reclaims a same-PID lock with no valid `starttime` whenever the process-local `HELD_LOCKS` map has no entry. The linked bug #71872 was closed after being cross-referenced, but the fix itself is not merged into main, and the branch’s latest provided diff also appears to have addressed the Greptile review nits.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #71903 open for maintainer review. The likely product/code direction is to preserve live same-PID locks when `starttime` cannot verify ownership, retain PID-recycle reclamation through `starttime` where available, and land focused regression coverage for the linked `sessions_history`/transcript-loss scenario or the narrow lock predicate before merging.
