## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not expose or document a `channels.slack.suppressAssistantText` option or an equivalent all-assistant-text suppression path. The shipped Slack behavior only narrows progress/preview noise; normal final assistant replies still go out, which matches the issue's gap. The linked PR #55223 is still relevant evidence rather than an already-landed fix.

## Evidence
