## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still supports DM/node pairing only; group handling remains allow/block through `open | disabled | allowlist`, with no group pairing state, owner approval/reject CLI, group-join owner notification, or pending read-only group-agent path. Related #8495 and PR #42400 are narrower and do not supersede the requested cross-channel group pairing flow.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product direction. The best path is an additive core/channel contract: add `groupPolicy: "pairing"` to config/schema/docs, persist pending group approvals keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, notify the owner on group join or first-seen unconfigured group, teach Telegram/WhatsApp and other group-capable channels to honor that state, and decide whether pending read-only observation belongs in the same implementation or a narrower follow-up coordinated with #8495 and PR #42400.
