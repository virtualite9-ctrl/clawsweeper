## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still has DM/node pairing only; group policy remains allow/block (`open | disabled | allowlist`), Telegram and WhatsApp group paths do not create pending owner approvals, and the `openclaw pairing` CLI approves DM sender codes into the allowFrom store rather than group chat IDs. Related #8495 and #42400 are useful context but remain open and narrower than the requested cross-channel group pairing flow.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product direction. The likely implementation is an additive core/channel contract: extend group policy config/schema/help/docs, add pending group approval state keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, notify the owner on group join or first-seen unconfigured group, teach Telegram/WhatsApp and other group-capable channels to honor it, and decide whether pending read-only observation belongs in the same change or in a narrower follow-up coordinated with #42400 and #8495.
