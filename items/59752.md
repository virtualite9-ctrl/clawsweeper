## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses blanket same-target suppression in both the immediate and followup reply paths, and current tests on `main` still assert that non-duplicate final replies should be dropped after a same-target messaging-tool send. The PR discussion also shows the branch is addressing a real, understood bug rather than an incoherent or obsolete request.
