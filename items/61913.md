## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the reported false-success cron announce path: isolated cron announce delivery now goes through the real outbound adapters, failed sends propagate as non-delivered/error outcomes, cron state persists `lastDelivered`/`lastDeliveryStatus` correctly, and ambiguous multi-channel announce configs are rejected up front. The changelog records the relevant fixes in `v2026.4.23`.

## Evidence
