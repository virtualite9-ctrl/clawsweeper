## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has related mitigations for timeout classification, undici timeout tuning, and oversized tool-result truncation, but I did not find strong evidence that issue #53540 is actually fixed on `main`, nor a reproduction-proof that the embedded runner no longer fails on large but still-valid OpenRouter payloads.
