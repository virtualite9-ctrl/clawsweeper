## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still enforces the sandboxed->unsandboxed `sessions_spawn` block, documents that guardrail, and does not expose the proposed `dangerouslyAllowUnsandboxedSubagentSpawn` config. This PR is a coherent, still-relevant core behavior change, not an implemented/stale/incoherent/clawhub close candidate.
