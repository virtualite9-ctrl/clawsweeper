## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes `imsg rpc` inbound text straight through the iMessage monitor without any tapback/reaction detection, while the BlueBubbles path still has dedicated tapback parsing. The linked discussion appears correct, and there is no evidence in `main` that the fix has landed.

## Evidence

- **iMessage monitor still forwards raw inbound text into decisioning:** `handleMessageNow()` derives `bodyText` directly from `message.text` (or a media placeholder) and immediately passes it into `resolveIMessageInboundDecision()` with no tapback/reaction prefilter.

  - file: [extensions/imessage/src/monitor/monitor-provider.ts:577](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/926068b14fc661e915a0247e9ecb5949fb95b4f7/extensions/imessage/src/monitor/monitor-provider.ts#L577)
