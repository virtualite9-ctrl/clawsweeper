## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the four proposed scanner rules, and the PR discussion surfaced unresolved correctness/noise issues in the proposed implementation that are consistent with how `scanSource()` currently evaluates context and source-wide matches.

None of `network-listener`, `prototype-pollution`, `symlink-manipulation`, or `credential-access` exist on current `main`.
