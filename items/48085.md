## Summary

Current `main` does not reproduce the reported `openclaw cron list` object-model crash on supported paths. Cron payload models are string-only by contract, non-string values are stripped during normalization, and persisted jobs are normalized before `cron.list` returns them. The PR also includes the ZenMux 402 change, which is already present on `main`.
