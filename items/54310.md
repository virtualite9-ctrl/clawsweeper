## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still ships `skills/canvas/SKILL.md` without YAML frontmatter, and the skill loader drops skills that lack a frontmatter `description`, so the reported discoverability bug remains real on `8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf`.
