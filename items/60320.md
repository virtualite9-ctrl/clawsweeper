## Summary

`/restart` now persists a session-scoped restart sentinel and automatically resumes the interrupted session after boot, with routing/thread context preserved. The implementation is present on current `main` and appears shipped in `v2026.4.23`.

## Evidence

- **Restart command now persists a resumable sentinel:** `buildRestartCommandSentinel` captures `sessionKey`, `deliveryContext`, `threadId`, and a default continuation message, and `handleRestartCommand` writes that sentinel before restart.
