## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has useful cancellation primitives, but it does not implement the issue's requested unified run-generation invalidation, stale-output fencing across visible emission sites, pre-tool skip gate, or new-message takeover semantics. The related draft PR #70363 is open and explicitly partial, so it is evidence that the direction is active rather than obsolete.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #70319 open as the umbrella until maintainers either land #70363 plus follow-ups or split the remaining work into narrower tracked issues. The product direction should be a session-scoped invalidation contract used by reply delivery, typing/progress/tool callbacks, embedded tool dispatch, subprocess scopes, follow-up delivery, and new-message handling, with tests proving stale runs cannot emit visible effects after `/stop` or takeover.
