## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still submits form-mode config by serializing the form object directly, while the gateway continues to reject literal `__OPENCLAW_REDACTED__` values. The linked fix PR #60971 was closed unmerged, and its stripping helper/regression coverage are not present on `main`.
