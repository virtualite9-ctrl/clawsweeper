## Summary

Keep open. Current main has the SecretRef foundation and env/file/exec providers, and documents 1Password through an exec provider recipe, but it still does not expose native keyring or native 1Password SecretRef/provider sources requested by #17311. The latest issue discussion also explicitly recommended keeping this open for that remaining narrower follow-through.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #17311 open until native keyring and 1Password SecretRef/provider sources are implemented across types, schemas, resolver/runtime, configure/config CLI UX, docs, and tests, or until a maintainer explicitly decides that exec recipes are the final supported direction and opens or links a narrower canonical follow-up for any remaining keyring/macOS Keychain work.
