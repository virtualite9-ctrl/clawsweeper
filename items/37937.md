## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats unclassified unhandled rejections as fatal, and the specific `undefined`/`null` non-fatal guard proposed in PR #37937 is not present on `main` at `55389f4be29359e2dd57ebaf548ba4a70331a445`. The associated regression tests are also absent, so this is not implemented on `main` and there is not strong evidence to close it for another reason.
