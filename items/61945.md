## Summary

The report is about a published third-party ClawHub skill (`imap-smtp-email`) and its own `smtp.js` HTML-send path, not behavior implemented in the OpenClaw core repo. Current `main` contains no bundled `imap-smtp-email` skill, no matching `smtp.js` script, and project policy/docs route new optional skills to ClawHub rather than core.

## Evidence
