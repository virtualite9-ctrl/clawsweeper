## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not apply a post-mutation `sanitizeToolUseResultPairing()` pass after the late transcript edits in `runEmbeddedAttempt`, and the newer outbound stream sanitizer does not reliably cover the unchanged-but-dangling `tool_use` case this PR targets.

## Evidence

- **Late transcript mutations still happen without a follow-up pairing repair:** After orphaned trailing-user cleanup, image pruning, and heartbeat filtering, `runEmbeddedAttempt` updates `activeSession.agent.state.messages` but does not re-run `sanitizeToolUseResultPairing()` in this late stage.
