## Summary

Current `main` does not include this PR's gateway origin fix. `src/gateway/origin-check.ts` still lowercases `url.origin` directly, so `chrome-extension://...` normalizes to `"null"` and exact allowlist matching still fails; the proposed extension-origin regression tests are also absent.
