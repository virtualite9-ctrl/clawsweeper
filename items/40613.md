## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes child-process stdout/stderr through bare `chunk.toString()` before exec aggregation, and the later notify/system-event path only normalizes whitespace. That means the Windows encoding failure described in #40613 is not clearly fixed on `main` and there is not strong evidence to close conservatively.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/process/supervisor/adapters/child.ts:111](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/b0d2003fe65bd7a9812f7c7f5ad9f608a7fd1503/src/process/supervisor/adapters/child.ts#L111)
