## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` shows the active auth/account label in `/models` output and supports manual typed profile overrides like `/model <provider/model>@<profile>`, but the `/models` interactive flow still only lets users choose provider and model. There is no account/auth-profile picker wired into the buttons/menu flow for GPT, Claude, or other multi-profile providers.

## Evidence
