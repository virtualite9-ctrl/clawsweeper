## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a shipped OpenClaw-side fix for ACPX orphaned child-process cleanup on Windows. The bundled ACPX extension is still a thin wrapper over the external `acpx` runtime, and its `cancel`/`close` paths only delegate to that runtime without any OpenClaw-specific process-tree termination. Given the issue is only 20 days old and this review was read-only, there is not enough evidence to conservatively close it as fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence
