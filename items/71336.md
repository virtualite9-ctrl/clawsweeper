## Summary

Do not close #71336 as conservative cleanup. Current main still keeps `chatQueue` in memory, clears it on session switch, and only flushes an already-present in-memory queue after seq-gap reconnect. The linked bug #51549 remains open, while prior maintainer discussion closed stale duplicate #56542 and split session-store recovery into #71328, leaving a focused WebChat persistence fix still relevant.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the item open, or replace it with a fresh focused WebChat PR if maintainers prefer. The right fix belongs in the UI chat/storage layer: persist queued outbound messages per session, restore and drain them on reconnect/session switch, clear storage on drain/removal/TTL expiry, add regression coverage around queue mutation and reconnect/session-switch paths, and keep the session-store recovery work separate under #71328.
