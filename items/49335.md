Keep open. Current `main` still synchronously reads and parses the full transcript on the `chat.history` path, so the reported freeze is not implemented away or obsolete. The PR targets a real hot-path problem, but the discussion also shows unresolved correctness risks around sequence stability and oversized-record handling, so there is not strong evidence to close it conservatively.
