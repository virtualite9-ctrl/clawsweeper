## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats both `openclaw cron status` and `OPENCLAW_NO_RESPAWN=1` as supported flows, and the provided timeline links a newer open 1006 repro (#62762). I found no high-confidence evidence in this checkout that the reported cron/non-direct invocation failure class is fixed well enough to close.
