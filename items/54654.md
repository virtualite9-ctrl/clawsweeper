## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not already contain this PR's Feishu empty-card handling, and the remaining `edit` path still appears to pass empty `{}` cards through to Feishu. The PR body's exact `send` reproduction is partly stale on current `main`, but that is not strong enough for `cannot_reproduce`, and it is clearly not `implemented_on_main`.
