## Summary

Current main contains the subagent-agent approval wiring this report needed, the changelog says the specific fix shipped in v2026.4.5, and the reporter later confirmed they had updated to 4.5. The remaining confusion in the thread is explained by exec-approvals files without `"version": 1` being treated as empty policy and by approvals being evaluated against the spawned child agent id.
