## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still reduces inbound WhatsApp videos to `<media:video>` and does not extract GIF/media metadata such as `gifPlayback` or `contextInfo.externalAdReply` fields, so the issue is not implemented on `main`.

## Evidence
