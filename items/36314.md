## Summary

Keep #36314 open. Current main has partial robustness mechanisms, including channel health restarts, platform supervisor restart limits, global unhandled rejection/exception handling, readiness probes, diagnostics/stability bundles, and last-known-good config recovery. The issue asks for a cohesive Gateway-level process watchdog, configurable recovery strategy map, richer health counters, and deliberate degraded startup mode; those remain only partially covered and are not represented as a first-class Gateway config/schema/docs surface.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the umbrella issue open or split it into focused maintainer-owned work: add a first-class gateway.watchdog config/schema/docs surface, define process restart-loop detection and notification semantics above platform supervisors, decide whether user-configurable global recovery strategies are wanted, enrich privacy-safe Gateway health metrics, and design degraded startup behavior that composes with last-known-good config and existing channel health recovery.
