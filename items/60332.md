## Summary

Keep open. On current `main` (`926068b14fc661e915a0247e9ecb5949fb95b4f7`), `/status` and `session_status` still render only the persisted `sessionEntry.compactionCount`, while the `ownsCompaction: true` context-engine path runs engine compaction, maintenance, hooks, and post-compaction side effects without incrementing or persisting that counter. The public `ContextEngine` contract also still has no stats surface for engines to report their own compaction totals.
