## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement per-account SOUL-file support, and the requested behavior still looks like a real missing feature. The current code/docs only support the workspace-level `SOUL.md`, while config writes are schema-validated against strict channel account schemas that do not accept a `soulFile` field.
