## Summary

Current main already rejects ambiguous announce delivery on the public `cron.add`/`cron.update` path, and the same behavior is present in the shipped `v2026.4.23` tree. This PR's service-layer change would be defense-in-depth for direct internal callers, but the reported create/update bug is already fixed well enough on main.
