## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes legacy `openclaw/plugin-sdk` imports through the compatibility loader, but that compatibility surface does not export `resolvePreferredOpenClawTmpDir`; the helper is only exposed on the focused `openclaw/plugin-sdk/temp-path` subpath. That is hard to square with the repo’s documented compatibility policy for deprecated SDK surfaces and with the WeChat docs, which currently advertise `@tencent-weixin/openclaw-weixin` 2.x as compatible with OpenClaw `>=2026.3.22`.
