## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has durable session-store/transcript persistence and a Feishu session-routing fix shipped in `2026.4.2`, but the available evidence does not prove this specific Feishu DM loss case is fixed or obsolete. The report says the missing turn never appeared in any transcript file, which could still be an ingress/delivery or session-routing gap; there is no direct current-main reproduction or targeted reconnect test that closes that loop.
