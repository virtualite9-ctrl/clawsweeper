## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements `sessions_send` as an internal `deliver: false` send followed by a separate announce flow, and the current routing logic/tests explicitly allow fresh inter-session targets with no established external route to adopt `webchat` delivery context. That matches the issue’s reported leak/rebind shape rather than showing a fix on `main`.
