## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has a durable outbound delivery queue for the generic outbound transport, but Telegram interactive/final reply delivery still goes through the legacy Telegram reply pipeline, which sends directly and only does short in-memory retries. That means the reported lost-final-message behavior is still plausible on `main`, and the issue also has a fresh repro against `v2026.4.22` in the provided context.

## Evidence

- **Telegram final replies bypass generic outbound queue:** `dispatchTelegramMessage` imports `deliverReplies` from the Telegram-local delivery module and uses it for normal payload sends and fallback final replies, rather than routing through `src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts`.
  - file: [extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.ts:54](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf/extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.ts#L54)
  - command: `nl -ba extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.ts | sed -n '45,70p;625,650p;1148,1187p'`
  - sha: [8f64cd3e4d83](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf)
- **Telegram reply delivery sends directly to bot API:** `deliverReplies` delivers text through `sendTelegramText(...)` and media through direct `bot.api.sendAnimation/sendPhoto/sendVideo/sendAudio/sendDocument` calls. There is no `enqueueDelivery`/`ackDelivery`/`failDelivery` usage in this path, so failed interactive replies are not persisted for later drain/recovery.
  - file: [extensions/telegram/src/bot/delivery.replies.ts:108](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf/extensions/telegram/src/bot/delivery.replies.ts#L108)

- **Durable queue exists elsewhere, but not on Telegram interactive path:** The generic outbound transport does implement a write-ahead delivery queue with `enqueueDelivery(...)` before send and `ackDelivery`/`failDelivery` cleanup after send. That confirms the durable mechanism exists in repo, but it is attached to `deliverOutboundPayloads`, not the Telegram reply pipeline above.
  - file: [src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:644](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf/src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts#L644)
