## Summary

Current `main` has meaningful mitigations for plan-only stalls and heartbeat interruption, but the evidence is not strong enough to say issue #63955 is fully resolved. The no-stall guard is explicitly scoped to strict-agentic GPT-5 OpenAI/Codex lanes, heartbeat wakes are protected from busy-session interruption, and normal interactive/heartbeat turns still do not get durable task-state tracking. Conservatively, this should stay open.
