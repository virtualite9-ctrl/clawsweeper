## Summary

Keep open. Current main has added a cron run-session reaper and documented `cron.sessionRetention`, so the indefinite-retention part is largely addressed. But #38730 also asks for guardrails/warnings, and the specific high-frequency cron warning remains absent on main while the related PR #38731 is still open and unmerged. A meaningful requested capability remains missing.
