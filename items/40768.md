## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still recognizes Feishu group mentions by exact `mention.id.open_id === botOpenId` matching against the startup-probed bot `open_id`, with no cross-app identity fallback. That means the reported app-scoped `open_id` mismatch is not implemented away on `main`, and I found no current docs/tests/changelog evidence that this specific multi-bot case was fixed.
