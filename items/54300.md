## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the thread-ownership path-construction issue: `extensions/thread-ownership/index.ts` interpolates `channelId` and `threadTs` directly into `/api/v1/ownership/${channelId}/${threadTs}` without `encodeURIComponent`, and its fallback normalizer returns non-canonical values unchanged. The other two findings are weaker for closure purposes: the Docker label helper is only called with the hardcoded label `openclaw.configHash`, and the plaintext-WS break-glass behavior is intentional, documented, tested, and called out in the changelog, so that portion is a design-hardening request rather than evidence that the report is obsolete.
