## Summary

Keep #39406 open. Current main still does not provide the requested normal-chat `toolErrorDelivery`/`all|final|off` policy or a global suppression option for transient mutating tool warnings after a successful final reply. The existing implementation has partial mitigations, but tests still encode that mutating tool errors are surfaced even with `messages.suppressToolErrors` and even after assistant output.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39406 open for a documented normal-chat tool-error delivery policy near `resolveToolErrorWarningPolicy` and `buildEmbeddedRunPayloads`. The best implementation should preserve the safe default, add config/schema/help/docs coverage for the chosen policy, keep unresolved mutating failures visible unless explicitly suppressed, and coordinate with #39631 and #51091 without treating unmerged PRs as shipped behavior.
