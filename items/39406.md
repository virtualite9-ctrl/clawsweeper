## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the requested global tool-error delivery policy. The only shipped config is `agents.defaults.heartbeat.suppressToolErrorWarnings`, and mutating tool failures are still intentionally surfaced after a successful assistant reply unless a per-run suppression flag is passed programmatically.
