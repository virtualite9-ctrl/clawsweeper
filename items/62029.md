## Summary

Keep open. The request is coherent, recent, and in scope for the bundled `memory-core` dreaming system. Current `main` still hard-filters deep-phase promotion candidates on `minScore` after the other gates, exposes no `wildcardRatio`-style option in the ranking API or plugin schema, and documents deep promotion as threshold-gated/internal behavior rather than including any wildcard sampling path.
