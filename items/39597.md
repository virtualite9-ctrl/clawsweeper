## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` distinguishes gateway clients at the connection layer and now threads client identity into chat turns as sender metadata, but the trusted agent/runtime context still only carries `channel=...` and does not expose a first-class webchat client type such as Mac app vs browser.

## Evidence
