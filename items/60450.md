## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has bounded per-run retry logic, same-provider failover caps, and auth/billing cooldown handling, but I did not find strong evidence that the reported cross-provider autonomous 401-spend loop is fully resolved. I also did not find a current global spend guard, cross-provider failover ceiling, or proactive auth-storm alert that would justify closing this conservatively.

## Evidence
