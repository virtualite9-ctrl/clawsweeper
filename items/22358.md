## Summary

Keep open. Current main has subagent lifecycle plugin hooks, but not the requested post-subagent-completion, pre-announcement hook that can inspect completion output, observe delivery status, and enrich/suppress/fallback the completion announcement.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #22358 open until OpenClaw either adds a public plugin hook at the post-completion/pre-announcement point with completion output, delivery result, and announcement mutation/suppression semantics, or a maintainer explicitly folds that exact scope into a canonical lifecycle/delivery design issue.
