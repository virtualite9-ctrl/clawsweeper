## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not implement Telegram pinned-message reads: Telegram actions expose send/react/delete/edit/sticker/topic flows and delivery pinning, but not `list-pins`, `/getpinned`, or a `getChat.pinned_message` reader. The linked PR attempts were closed unmerged, and the referenced commit is not present in this checkout.
