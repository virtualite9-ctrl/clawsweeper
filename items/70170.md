## Summary

Current `main` still appears to exclude DuckDuckGo from onboarding setup. `src/flows/search-setup.ts` only shows providers with `onboardingScopes: ["text-inference"]`, but DuckDuckGo's shared provider base and manifest on `main` do not supply that metadata. This PR is addressing a real current gap, although its head also includes broader `ddg-client` behavior changes that need normal maintainer review rather than conservative auto-closure.
