## Summary

Current `main` has clear prompt-cache stability work for dynamic instructions, but I did not find strong evidence that the same session now guarantees identical tool exposure across direct vs inter-session turns. The instruction-prefix half of the report looks substantially mitigated; the tool-drift half is not disproven strongly enough to close conservatively.

## Evidence
