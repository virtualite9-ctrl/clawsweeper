## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has several adjacent mitigations for stuck tool behavior, but they are piecemeal and do not amount to the proposed end-to-end self-healing execution flow. The repo shows optional loop detection, an always-on unknown-tool guard, limited malformed tool-call argument repair, compaction summaries of past tool failures, and a few narrow retry/fallback paths. It does not show the requested unified classifier/planner/verifier/state-guard design or a general default policy that stops deterministic tool-validation loops and preserves per-turn failure state outside compaction.
