## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has loopback pairing recovery improvements that address the later comments, but I did not find strong evidence that the original reported regression (`exec` + `/usr/bin/crontab` getting immediate `SIGKILL`/timeouts after 2026.4.9) is fixed, intentionally unsupported, or obsolete.

## Evidence

- **Issue discussion shifted from exec failure to pairing UX:** The issue body reports `exec` calls to `/usr/bin/crontab` timing out or dying with `SIGKILL`, while the follow-up comments pivot to device-pairing friction for `openclaw cron add` and ask for CLI/token-based approval paths. That means the thread now mixes at least two different problems, so the pairing fixes on `main` are not enough by themselves to close the original exec regression.
