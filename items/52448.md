## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `8866544ffefb42ca234c66c337134fb60743d8d0` still only seeds the bootstrap markdown files into sandbox workspaces; it does not materialize allowed top-level authority links such as `README.md`, `CLAUDE.md`, `docs`, or `voro-docs`. The PR’s goal is therefore not already implemented on `main`, and the review thread still shows unresolved sandbox-boundary questions in the proposed copy logic, so there is not strong evidence to close it conservatively.

## Evidence

There is no logic here to walk or materialize top-level authority symlinks.
