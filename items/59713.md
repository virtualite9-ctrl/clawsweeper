## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still preloads the full plugin registry for `channels` commands, including `login`/`logout`, while explicit-channel auth still resolves the target plugin on demand. The PRâ€™s underlying optimization is not implemented on `main` and remains relevant, though it now needs rebasing onto the newer command-catalog/startup-policy structure.
