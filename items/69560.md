## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR’s runtime guard in quickstart channel selection, and the undefined-selection path is still plausible through the generic wizard session/RPC surface even though the standard CLI prompter cancels by throwing instead of returning `undefined`. That is not strong enough evidence for an auto-close.
