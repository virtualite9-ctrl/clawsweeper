## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks the symlink-discovery change, and the PR’s install-root change is not safe to auto-close or treat as already resolved because it appears to diverge from the repo’s current `resolveConfigDir`-based discovery contract when `OPENCLAW_CONFIG_PATH` is used.
