## Summary

Keep #20802 open. Current main still treats `llm_input`, `llm_output`, and `after_tool_call` as observation-only hooks, and the embedded attempt and tool-adapter paths do not await or apply returned hook mutations. Related work overlaps with the `llm_input`/`llm_output` part, but the adapter-side `after_tool_call` result-mutation question remains unsuperseded and still needs maintainer/API judgment.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20802 open unless a maintainer explicitly rejects the broader direction or lands a canonical replacement. Prefer reviewing or landing the focused `llm_input`/`llm_output` work through #61485 or another narrow PR, then split adapter-side `after_tool_call` result mutation into a separate API decision that accounts for existing bundled-only tool-result middleware and exactly-once hook dispatch tests.
