## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats exec allowlists as binary-path entries, not user-configurable subcommand/argv patterns. There is partial internal support for approval-derived exact-command durability, but that is not the feature requested here and does not provide a general `openclaw cron *` style allowlist surface.
