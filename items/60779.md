## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not already contain these changes, and the submitted PR head introduces concrete syntax/control-flow regressions called out in review. At least one claimed fix is also weakly supported: the current env-var regex does not match `${name}` or `${NAME}` in a direct check, so the PR is not a strong close candidate under any allowed reason.
