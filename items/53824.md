## Summary

Current `main` already contains a Discord recipient-resolution/send-path implementation that fixes `user:<id>` delivery by resolving the recipient and explicitly opening the DM channel before send. Isolated cron announce delivery flows through that outbound adapter path, and the fix shipped in `v2026.4.22` at `00bd2cf7a376f1fba26291c6c4766f1f15cbdfa5`.
