## Summary

Keep open. #45102 is a concrete, recent gateway-liveness bug report for Windows with local FunASR voice-recognition load, and current main does not show a default-on fix that would prevent or recover from the reported slowdown and /v1/responses timeouts. Related #64767 and #71127 are useful adjacent evidence, but both remain open and do not fully supersede this Windows/FunASR path.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #45102 open. Maintainers should reproduce or instrument the Windows/FunASR media path, capture gateway memory/event-loop behavior during repeated voice requests, and then either fix the local ASR execution path directly or link it to a canonical shipped fix. Likely work belongs in gateway/media-understanding reliability: bounded local ASR execution, event-loop liveness diagnostics, safer transcript readers, and a conservative stuck-session recovery policy coordinated with #64767 and #71127.
