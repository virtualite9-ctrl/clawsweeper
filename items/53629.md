## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` only has the Telegram business update types in `allowed-updates`, but it still lacks the business message/business connection handler wiring, dedupe support, and outbound `business_connection_id` injection described by the PR. The PR also has unresolved correctness blockers from review, so there is not strong evidence to close it as already implemented or obsolete.
