## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still drops deliberate `NO_REPLY` payloads before delivery, but both downstream logging surfaces continue to treat the resulting empty payload list as a generic no-reply case, and `EmbeddedPiRunMeta` still has no `silentCompletion` marker to distinguish intentional silence.
