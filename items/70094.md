## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include the requested SIGTERM cleanup change, and the PR as submitted is not a safe conservative cleanup: it swaps supervisor cancellation to SIGTERM without restoring a hard-kill fallback, while also bundling many unrelated changes into the same review.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/session-write-lock.ts:39](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/2cd2732ab6695a4b3220ce41166b74aca7097c5f/src/agents/session-write-lock.ts#L39)
