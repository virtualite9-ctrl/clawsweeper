## Summary

Keep #25592 open. Current main has meaningful commentary/final_answer hardening, but it still preserves channel-facing delivery for unphased assistant text before tool execution and has no replyMode/tool-only or configurable final-only policy. The issue discussion also explicitly treats #25592 as the broader canonical bug after the commentary-phase subset fixes.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #25592 open as the canonical product bug until OpenClaw implements or explicitly rejects a channel-safe policy such as final-only delivery, opt-in replyMode/tool-only explicit sends, or configurable enforceFinalTag for channel sessions. Preserve the shipped commentary-phase hardening, but add coverage for unphased pre-tool/intermediate text so channel delivery cannot expose it.
