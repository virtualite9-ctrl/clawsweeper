## Summary

Current `main` now closes run-mode subagent runtimes on cleanup, marks completed runs so restart resume skips them, and sweeps completed entries with TTL/archive cleanup. The added persistence and lifecycle tests cover the restart/cleanup paths that match this report.

## Evidence
