## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `8866544ffefb42ca234c66c337134fb60743d8d0` still auto-threads Google Chat monitor replies, and the shipped config/docs only expose `replyToMode`, not a separate switch to suppress monitor-driven threading. The report matches current code.
