## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` clearly expects the dashboard at `/` by default and has tests covering SPA fallback routing, including `GET /dashboard`, plus new pack/prepack guards to prevent shipping a broken Control UI bundle. But I do not have a direct reproduction of the reporter’s `2026.3.7` failure, no install-method/config details, and no specific fixing commit tying this report to a shipped regression fix. That is not strong enough for a conservative close.
