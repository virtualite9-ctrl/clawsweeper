## Summary

Close #69445 as superseded by the already-open #10005. Current main still lacks per-agent or per-status-section suppression for `/status` and `session_status`, but #10005 is the canonical open tracker for adding status-card suppression on those exact chat/tool surfaces. The final design for #10005 should explicitly cover this issue's per-agent usage/billing privacy case.

## Best Possible Solution

Close #69445 as duplicate/superseded and keep the remaining product/code work on #10005. The eventual implementation should add a documented status-card visibility contract for chat `/status` and `session_status`, with schema/types/help/docs and regression coverage for per-agent usage-line suppression or a more general section visibility model.
