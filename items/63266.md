## Summary

Keep #63266 open. Current main has adjacent workspace-status and legacy-state migration plumbing, but it does not detect or repair the reported `workspace-local` parent plus `workspace-local/local` active-workspace split, and the migration/secrets docs do not give operators specific guidance for secrets left one level above the active workspace.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and address it in the workspace/doctor surface: teach `detectLegacyWorkspaceDirs()` or a new doctor check to detect active workspace paths nested under a plausible former workspace root, report parent-level `secrets/` or config-like directories/files, and offer a conservative `doctor --fix` or documented manual migration path. The docs should clarify canonical secret storage after Docker-to-host migration, preferably steering static credentials to SecretRef providers or state-dir-backed `credentials/`/`secrets.json` rather than workspace-local secrets.
