## Summary

Keep #36630 open. Current main at 406ae72fd2789c78053f6b94006ec8f488bc1c00 still lacks the PR's central bidirectional Signal quote-reply behavior: Signal sends do not emit signal-cli quote metadata, Signal outbound adapters do not forward quote-author/reply metadata, and inbound Signal quote context still omits ReplyToId plus the richer quote payload model proposed by the PR.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #36630 open. The best maintainer path is to land a reviewed, hardened version of this Signal quote-reply work or reimplement it narrowly: safe decimal Signal quote timestamp/id validation, group quote-author resolution from the referenced message, quote-aware Signal outbound delivery through formatted/plugin/monitor/queue paths, inbound ReplyToId plus richer signal-cli quote metadata, extension-boundary-compliant imports, and focused regression coverage for delivery consumption, debounce, queued/recovered delivery, and plugin-boundary cases.
