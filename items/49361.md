## Summary

Current `main` still has the Google Chat typing-indicator fallback chain `account.config.name -> agent.name -> "OpenClaw"`; it does not consult `agent.identity.name`, even though `identity.name` is a documented/configured agent identity field. The PR remains a live, narrowly scoped fix rather than cleanup-close material.
