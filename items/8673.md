## Summary

Keep #8673 open. Current main still performs OAuth refresh as a single shared refresh call guarded by locks/timeouts, but without bounded retry for transient network, timeout, 429, or 5xx failures. The only retry-like path is the separate `refresh_token_reused` recovery, and the two linked retry PRs were closed unmerged.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #8673 open and implement a bounded, tested retry policy around the current OAuth refresh operation. Prefer reusing `src/infra/retry.ts`, classify transient network/timeout/429/5xx-style refresh failures, avoid retrying permanent OAuth errors, preserve the existing `refresh_token_reused` recovery and main-store adoption behavior, and add focused tests under `src/agents/auth-profiles/` for success after a transient failure, retry exhaustion preserving the final error, and non-retryable OAuth failures.
