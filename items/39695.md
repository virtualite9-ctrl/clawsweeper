## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` shows partial mitigation and changed defaults, but not strong proof that the reported Discord-bound failure is fixed end-to-end for existing installs. `exec` is still a `coding`-profile tool, new local onboarding only defaults future configs to `coding`, and the legacy-config migration path I inspected does not show a matching `tools.profile` upgrade or a specific fix for missing channel session transcripts.
