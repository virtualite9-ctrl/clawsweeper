## Summary

Keep #37816 open. Current main has Telegram preview streaming, tool-progress preview lines, /verbose tool bubbles, and /reasoning visibility, but it does not provide the requested full persistent per-activity Telegram mode or config such as streaming:"full"/activityMessages:true. The related items in the issue body cover narrower verbose/streaming/NO_REPLY bugs and do not supersede this feature request.
