## Summary

Current `main` and release `v2026.4.23` already contain the status/session bookkeeping needed for sparse channel sessions to recover transcript-backed usage/model identity and resolve Anthropic Opus `context1m` at 1,048,576 tokens, with regression coverage matching the reported mis-accounting pattern.
