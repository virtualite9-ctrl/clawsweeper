## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the reported restart/reply race: gateway restarts now wait for pending reply dispatchers, outbound delivery is durably queued and recovered after restart, and restart-sentinel routing preserves the prior delivery context/thread for post-restart continuity.

## Evidence
