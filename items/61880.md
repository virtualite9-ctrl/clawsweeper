## Summary

Current main no longer routes generated videos through the media store's implicit 5 MB default. `video_generate` now passes the configured agent media limit into `saveMediaBuffer(...)`, with matching regression coverage, and the changelog records the broader generated-media/store fix as shipped in v2026.4.14.

## Evidence
