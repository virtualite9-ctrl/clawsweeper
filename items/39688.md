## Summary

Current `main` still does not deliver `event.messages` from internal `message:received` or `message:sent` hooks back to the user. The hook contract and docs say those messages are for user delivery, but the message hook paths only fire `triggerInternalHook(...)` and never inspect or route the populated `messages` array.
