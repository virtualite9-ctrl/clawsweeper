## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed provider-specific OpenAI/Azure concurrency limiter or the `LLM_MAX_CONCURRENT` env var, and the PR discussion documents unresolved correctness issues in the submitted patch. No conservative close reason applies.
