## Summary

Current main already routes queued replies through the outbound payload normalization pipeline that strips inline reply directives, preserves reply metadata, and skips empty/non-renderable sends. The routed-path and outbound-payload tests cover the exact leakage/empty-send behaviors described here.

## Evidence

- **Routed replies feed the outbound delivery pipeline:** `routeReply()` forwards routed payloads to `deliverOutboundPayloads()`, so routed sends do not bypass the shared outbound delivery path.
  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/route-reply.ts:228](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/926068b14fc661e915a0247e9ecb5949fb95b4f7/src/auto-reply/reply/route-reply.ts#L228)

  - file: [src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:729](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/926068b14fc661e915a0247e9ecb5949fb95b4f7/src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts#L729)
