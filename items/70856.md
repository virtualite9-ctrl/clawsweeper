## Summary

Keep open. Current main still only solves the narrower quiet-session watchdog part via the #70678 transport-activity fix. This issue reports broader WhatsApp instability that remains plausible in current code: retryable 408/428 reconnect churn, outbound sends failing during listener gaps, health-monitor restarts while WhatsApp is already reconnecting, and unrecovered group hydration failures after `groupFetchAllParticipating` errors.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open as a real WhatsApp plugin regression. Preserve the shipped #70678 transport-activity watchdog fix, then add focused fixes and coverage for retryable 408/428 reconnect churn, health-monitor coordination while WhatsApp is already reconnecting, short reconnect-gap outbound delivery queuing or waiting, and recovery from `groupFetchAllParticipating` 1006 plus libsignal/Bad MAC failures so group events and sends do not silently disappear.
