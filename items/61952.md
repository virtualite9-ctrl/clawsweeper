## Summary

Close as implemented on main. Current heartbeat handling suppresses ack-only `HEARTBEAT_OK` replies, filters heartbeat noise out of later cron/system-event prompts, and the same implementation is already present in the shipped `v2026.4.23` release.

## Evidence

- **Ack-only heartbeat replies are normalized and skipped:** `runHeartbeatOnce` normalizes the heartbeat reply, treats ack-only/no-media results as `shouldSkipMain`, restores the prior timestamp, emits an `ok-token` event, and returns instead of delivering or continuing with a visible reply.
