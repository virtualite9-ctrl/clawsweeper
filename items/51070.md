## Summary

Close #51070 as superseded by the broader service-identity/trusted-proxy auth discussion in #69066. Current main intentionally rejects loopback-source trusted-proxy auth, tests and docs codify that fail-closed contract, and maintainer feedback on the PR objected to a blanket loopback `{ method: "none" }` bypass because it removes identity and auditability.

## Best Possible Solution

Close #51070 as superseded. Keep the current fail-closed trusted-proxy contract on main, and continue any needed local/internal-caller relief through the canonical service-identity discussion in #69066 or narrower maintainer-approved fallback PRs that preserve identity and auditability.
