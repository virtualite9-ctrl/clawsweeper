## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still does not implement Telegram Business message support beyond allowing the update names through Telegram polling/webhook configuration; there are no business_message/business_connection handlers, no business payload dedupe or sequential-lane support, no outbound business_connection_id handling, and no user-facing Telegram Business docs/tests. The provided discussion and related items identify #20786 as the canonical tracker, with PR #53629 still only implementation context rather than landed main behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent Telegram plugin change in extensions/telegram that registers business_connection and business_message handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, handles dedupe/sequential keys, persists or maps business_connection_id for outbound replies while excluding unsupported calls such as sendChatAction, and adds focused tests plus docs on https://docs.openclaw.ai/channels/telegram.
