## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still does not implement Telegram Business message handling: business update names are present in allowed update selection, but there are no handlers, dedupe keys, sequential lanes, outbound business_connection_id mapping, docs, or tests for business_message/business_connection. The issue also has an explicit maintainer Codex review comment saying to keep it open, and PR #53629 remains the related implementation attempt rather than shipped behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent extensions/telegram implementation that registers business_connection and business_message handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, handles update dedupe and sequential lanes, persists/maps business_connection_id for outbound replies/actions, and adds focused tests plus user-facing Telegram docs.
