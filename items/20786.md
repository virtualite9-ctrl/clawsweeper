## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still does not implement Telegram Business Bot support: business update types are allowed through polling/webhook selection, but business_message/business_connection updates are not handled, deduped, sequenced, documented, or wired into outbound replies with business_connection_id. The related PR #53629 remains implementation context rather than evidence of a landed fix in this checkout.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Review and land PR #53629 or an equivalent Telegram plugin change that registers `business_connection` and `business_message` handlers, routes business messages through the inbound pipeline, adds dedupe/sequential-key support, persists/maps `business_connection_id`, injects it for outbound business replies while excluding unsupported calls such as `sendChatAction`, and adds focused tests plus docs for the Telegram Business behavior.
