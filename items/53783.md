## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` makes cross-agent `sessions_list`/`sessions_send` visibility explicitly requester-scoped, not Telegram-group-scoped, so the reported one-way behavior is consistent with a config mismatch (`tools.sessions.visibility` / `tools.agentToAgent`) rather than a confirmed Telegram bug. But the issue body does not include the relevant config or logs, and I could not do a live reproduction in this checkout, so there is not strong enough evidence to close conservatively.
