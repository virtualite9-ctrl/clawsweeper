## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still sends outbound WhatsApp media through Baileys, but the only Baileys patch on `main` is for encrypted-file flush timing and fetch dispatcher handling, not failure-path temp-file cleanup. I found no source, changelog entry, or regression test showing orphaned encrypted upload temp files are deleted after upload failure.
