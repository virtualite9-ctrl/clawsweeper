## Summary

Current `main` still closes the WhatsApp inbound monitor by calling the raw WebSocket `close()` path, not `sock.end()`. The teardown helper and its tests both encode that behavior, so the reported bug is not implemented/fixed on `main` and should remain open.

## Evidence
