## Summary

Keep open. Current main still copies session transcript text into session-memory files without sanitizing chat-template tokens, raw or dangling `<tool_call>` XML, role-marker leakage, or `NO_REPLY` turns. The PR addresses a real bundled-hook bug tracked by #69943, and the overlapping #71257 is not evidence of a shipped superseding fix.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #69961 open and consolidate it with #69943 and #71257 into one maintainer-reviewed session-memory sanitizer fix. The final implementation should sanitize or elide artifacts before persistence, preferably reuse or align with `src/shared/text/assistant-visible-text.ts`, cover `NO_REPLY`, chat-template tokens, raw and dangling tool-call XML, role-marker leakage, and false-positive cases in transcript/handler tests, then close #69943 after merge.
