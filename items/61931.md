## Summary

The reported exec/PTY late-stdout crash is already guarded on current `main`, and the same guard is present in the shipped `v2026.4.23` release.

## Evidence

- **Late update guard in exec runtime:** `emitUpdate()` now returns when `updatesDisabled` is set, in addition to the existing backgrounded/exited checks. This is the direct guard against late `tool_execution_update` callbacks reaching a disposed run.
