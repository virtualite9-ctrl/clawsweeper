## Summary

The reported root cause is contradicted by the current Discord channel entry contract: `registerFull(...)` is a supplemental full-mode hook wrapped inside a normal `register(...)` export, so the issue’s central diagnosis is incorrect and the report lacks logs or artifact-level evidence for any separate bug.
