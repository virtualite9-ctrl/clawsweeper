## Summary

Current `main` already applies inbound audio transcription as a general media-understanding step before agent dispatch, and Telegram DMs also have explicit voice-note preflight coverage. The changelog records the DM placeholder fix in release `2026.4.5`, so this issue is obsolete on current OpenClaw.

## Evidence
