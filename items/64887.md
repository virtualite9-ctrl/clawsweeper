## Summary

Keep open. Current main still keeps `livenessState` in embedded-run metadata and lifecycle events, while the final user-facing reply payload contract and delivery path do not carry a structured `livenessState` field or stable blocked/abandoned footer.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open and implement a generic surfacing layer. When final embedded-run `meta.livenessState` is `blocked` or `abandoned`, ensure the final user-facing payload carries that state either through a documented `ReplyPayload`/`AgentRuntimeReplyPayload` field or a stable footer such as `Run ended early: blocked.` Add focused regressions for strict blocked and incomplete abandoned exits, plus one delivery-path test proving a channel or CLI-style renderer receives the surfaced state.
