## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `22f23fa5ab6f625835bfc3dbaba73c200775027c` does not implement the requested `tools.web.fetch.allowPrivateNetwork` opt-in or an equivalent private-network override for `web_fetch`. The only exposed `web_fetch` SSRF override is `ssrfPolicy.allowRfc2544BenchmarkRange`, while localhost/private IP blocking remains the tested and documented behavior.
