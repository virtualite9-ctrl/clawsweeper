## Summary

Current `main` already supports the requested self-hosted behavior well enough: WhatsApp outbound target resolution now allows any normalized E.164 when `allowFrom` is unset/empty, while inbound DM access remains controlled separately by `channels.whatsapp.dmPolicy` and pairing approvals.

## Evidence
