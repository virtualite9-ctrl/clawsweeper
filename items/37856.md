## Summary

Current `main` does not contain the provider-stall observability added by PR #37856. The embedded subscribe params/state do not carry provider/model or stall-baseline fields, tool/lifecycle handlers do not emit a pre-tool/pre-end stall warning, and there is no `pi-embedded-subscribe.provider-stall.ts` implementation on `main`.
