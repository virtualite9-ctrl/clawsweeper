## Summary

Current `main` already supports the requested outcome: bundled skills can be globally allowlisted, individually disabled, or hidden per agent, and runtime filtering keeps blocked/disabled skills out of the agent-visible prompt. Config storage is per-skill override data, not a required full list of bundled skills.
