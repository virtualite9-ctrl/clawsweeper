## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still compares pinned TLS fingerprints with plain `!==` in both gateway paths, so this is not implemented on `main`, and the repo evidence does not strongly disprove the security-hardening claim. The PR is small and plausible, but it does not meet the maintainer bug-fix evidence bar strongly enough to justify an auto-close.
