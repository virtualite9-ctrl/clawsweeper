## Summary

The issue is a community notice about Anthropic billing classification, legal complaints, and external workarounds rather than a remaining OpenClaw repo change. Current main already covers the OpenClaw-owned side of the Claude CLI path, and the remaining dispute is with Anthropic billing/policy or an external proxy workaround.

## Best Possible Solution

Close this broad community/legal notice as outside the OpenClaw source repo. Keep the shipped Claude CLI/API-key behavior and docs, and handle any fresh current-main 402 failure as a new focused bug or as external proxy/provider-policy work outside core.
