## Summary

Current `main` already supports Discord bot-triggered activation through existing config: `channels.discord.allowBots` enables bot-authored inbound messages, `"mentions"` gates them on real bot mentions, and guild/channel `users` allowlists can restrict that behavior to specific Discord IDs, including bot accounts.
