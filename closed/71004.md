## Summary

Current main implements the RFC’s central contract-first RuntimePlan boundary: OpenClaw-owned auth, prompt, tools, transcript, delivery, outcome, transport, and observability policy are represented in AgentRuntimePlan, built before attempts, and consumed by Pi/Codex paths and the additive harness V2 lifecycle. The policy-domain contract tests from the RFC ladder are present, and the shipped changelog records #71096 in v2026.4.24.

## Best Possible Solution

Close the umbrella RFC and keep the shipped RuntimePlan/Harness V2 implementation. Any remaining optional refactors should be handled as focused follow-ups against the specific policy domain or harness API surface.
