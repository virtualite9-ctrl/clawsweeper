## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the reported Telegram multi-bot `getUpdates` conflict path: each started Telegram account gets its own polling monitor/transport, polling marks the transport dirty and rebuilds on `409 Conflict`, transport creation forces HTTP/1.1 with bounded undici pools, and current docs/runtime guard against duplicate-token/default-account multi-account misconfiguration. The changelog shows the polling transport fix shipped in `2026.4.18`.

## Evidence
