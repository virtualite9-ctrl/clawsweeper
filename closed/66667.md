## Summary

Current `main` already exposes supported pre-delivery reply interception through plugin hooks: `message_sending` can rewrite or cancel outbound reply text before send, and `reply_dispatch` can own the final reply-dispatch pipeline. A separate `before_delivery` hook would duplicate existing supported surfaces.

## Evidence
