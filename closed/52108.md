## Summary

Current `main` already provides a pre-execution tool interception seam through plugin `before_tool_call` hooks, including block/modify/approval behavior before `exec` or other tools run, and it shipped in `v2026.4.22` (`00bd2cf7a376f1fba26291c6c4766f1f15cbdfa5`).
