## Summary

Current `main` still treats MiniMax image understanding as a separate `MiniMax-VL-01` media path, not as `MiniMax-M2.7`. The reported behavior remains actionable: code, tests, and docs all codify the opposite of the requested fix, and the issue timeline already points to still-open follow-up work.
