## Summary

Current main implements the user-visible video analysis workflow through the shared media-understanding provider surface. Qwen now registers image and video media understanding with qwen-vl-max-latest, video auto-selection includes Qwen via provider metadata, Telegram video attachments flow into shared media handling, and checked-in tests cover video request construction and application into the reply context. The exact proposed models[].input "video" enum was not added, but the observable request is covered well enough by the implemented media-understanding path.
