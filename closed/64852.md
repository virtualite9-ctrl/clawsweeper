## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still resolves `google-vertex` ADC/service-account auth to the non-secret `gcp-vertex-credentials` marker, and I did not find a bundled Google runtime-auth exchange that would turn that marker into a bearer credential before the Google transport falls back to `x-goog-api-key` handling. That matches the reported 401/CREDENTIALS_MISSING failure mode more than an already-fixed issue.
