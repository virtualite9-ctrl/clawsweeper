## Summary

Current OpenClaw already covers the requested low-latency iMessage path: the legacy `imessage` integration is event-driven via `imsg rpc` + `watch.subscribe`, and the bundled/recommended BlueBubbles channel provides webhook-based inbound delivery for near-real-time behavior.

## Evidence
