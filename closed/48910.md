## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still resolves `google-vertex` ADC to the synthetic `gcp-vertex-credentials` marker and then feeds that marker into the bundled Google transport as `options.apiKey`, where it is serialized as `x-goog-api-key` instead of becoming OAuth/ADC auth. The separate `gaxios` compatibility part looks partially addressed on newer main, but the primary `google-vertex` auth bug is not implemented away.

## Evidence
