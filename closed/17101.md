## Summary

Current `main` already implements the reported Telegram voice-note transcription behavior: inbound DM audio is preflight-transcribed and the raw `<media:audio>` placeholder is replaced with transcript text. The changelog shows this shipped in `v2026.4.21`, and a later commenter on the issue confirmed that release fixed the original symptom.
