## Summary

Current `main` already contains Telegram media-download handling for the reported SSRF/private-IP block: Telegram media fetches now always allow RFC2544 fake-IP answers, support explicit `apiRoot` overrides, and expose a Telegram-only `network.dangerouslyAllowPrivateNetwork` opt-in for trusted fake-IP/transparent-proxy environments. The behavior is covered by tests, documented for users, and called out in the changelog as shipped in `v2026.4.5`.

## Evidence
