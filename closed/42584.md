## Summary

Current `main` already prevents heartbeat/cron/exec turns from rewriting session origin and delivery metadata, and the session list still derives titles from preserved `origin.label`. The PR's `ConversationLabel` addition in `src/infra/heartbeat-runner.ts` is no longer necessary on top of the newer session-state logic.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/session.ts:234](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/459d277076488d5833a181cca7d8bf5c0f9696dc/src/auto-reply/reply/session.ts#L234)

  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/session.ts:533](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/459d277076488d5833a181cca7d8bf5c0f9696dc/src/auto-reply/reply/session.ts#L533)
