## Summary

Current `main` already routes Discord `send` actions with `filePath`/`media` through the media-aware path, promotes `caption` when `message` is blank, and the Discord sender explicitly supports attachment sends with no text content.

## Evidence

- **Core send parsing now accepts filePath/path/media and caption fallback:** `handleSendAction` reads `media`, `mediaUrl`, `path`, `filePath`, and `fileUrl` as attachment hints, promotes `caption` into `message` when the message is blank, and only rejects the send when both text and media are absent.
