## Summary

Current main and the v2026.4.24 release contain browser CDP contention safeguards that address the reported mismatch: stale Chromium Singleton locks are cleaned and retried, browser startup waits for CDP websocket readiness before reporting success, failures surface as explicit errors, and concurrent lazy starts are deduplicated.

## Evidence
