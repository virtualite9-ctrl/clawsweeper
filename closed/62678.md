## Summary

The reported webchat/control-ui leak is implemented on current `main`: OpenAI/Codex responses are handled with assistant phases, Control UI renders only visible assistant text, and `chat.history` now strips commentary-only/scaffolding content. The changelog indicates this shipped after the reporter's `2026.3.13` build.
