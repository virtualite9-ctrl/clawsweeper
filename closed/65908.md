## Summary

Current `main` now resolves QMD searches from the live managed collection set, rebinding stale path+pattern conflicts to the current collection name, retrying missing-collection searches after repair, avoiding phantom `memory-alt` queries, and preserving explicit external custom collection names. That covers the remaining scope left open in #65908 after the earlier partial fix noted in the discussion.
