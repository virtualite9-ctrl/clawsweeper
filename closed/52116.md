## Summary

Current `main` already includes the Telegram polling transport recovery work this issue asks for: dirty polling cycles rebuild the transport, stale dispatchers are explicitly closed, polling exit disposes the held transport, and the changelog records that fix as shipped in 2026.4.18.

## Evidence

- **Polling session now disposes transport on exit and marks dirty cycles for rebuild:** `TelegramPollingSession` disposes the transport in `runUntilAbort()`, marks the transport dirty on stall restarts and recoverable polling errors, and uses that dirty flag before the next polling cycle.
  - file: [extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:121](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/60f7a59f5ed581839cfd1d52583449c5aa1f03a4/extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts#L121)

- **Dirty rebuild closes stale transport instead of reusing poisoned dispatcher pools:** `TelegramPollingTransportState.acquireForNextCycle()` closes the previous transport when a dirty rebuild replaces it, with comments describing the prior accumulation of dead keep-alive sockets after recoverable network errors and stall restarts.
  - file: [extensions/telegram/src/polling-transport-state.ts:22](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/60f7a59f5ed581839cfd1d52583449c5aa1f03a4/extensions/telegram/src/polling-transport-state.ts#L22)
