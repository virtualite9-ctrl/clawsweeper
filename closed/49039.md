## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still appears to route `google-vertex` ADC auth through the Google Gemini transport as a literal non-JSON `apiKey`, which the transport converts into `x-goog-api-key` headers instead of bearer auth.

## Evidence

- **google-vertex ADC still resolves to a sentinel marker:** `resolveGoogleVertexEnvApiKey()` returns `GCP_VERTEX_CREDENTIALS_MARKER` when `GOOGLE_CLOUD_PROJECT`/`GOOGLE_CLOUD_LOCATION` and ADC credentials are present, and `resolveEnvApiKey()` returns that marker for provider `google-vertex`.
