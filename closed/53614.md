The runtime subagent bootstrap allowlist includes `USER.md` alongside `AGENTS.md`, `TOOLS.md`, `SOUL.md`, and `IDENTITY.md` in `src/agents/workspace.ts:544`, and the test coverage in `src/agents/workspace.test.ts:64` / `src/agents/workspace.test.ts:305` explicitly expects `USER.md` to be available for subagent sessions.
