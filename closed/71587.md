## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already covers most of the report: OpenClaw now documents and tests `opencode-go/kimi-k2.6`, `glm-5.1`, `mimo-v2-*`, and `qwen3.*` in the bundled Go catalog. But the current bundled `opencode-go` catalog still does not include `opencode-go/deepseek-v4-flash` or `opencode-go/deepseek-v4-pro`, so the issue remains partially valid and should stay open until the remaining Go DeepSeek entries are actually present.
