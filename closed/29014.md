## Summary

Current main implements the core requested behavior: overloaded/529/high-demand/api_error/5xx provider failures are failover-classified, bounded same-provider retry/profile rotation is configurable, and exhausted overloaded attempts advance into the configured model fallback chain instead of hanging.
