  - file: [src/gateway/server-methods/nodes.ts:565](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/e23d17da79dc2345ca55902e632d3c7622accacc/src/gateway/server-methods/nodes.ts#L565)

  - file: [src/gateway/node-catalog.ts:117](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/e23d17da79dc2345ca55902e632d3c7622accacc/src/gateway/node-catalog.ts#L117)

  - file: [docs/nodes/index.md:44](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/e23d17da79dc2345ca55902e632d3c7622accacc/docs/nodes/index.md#L44)
