## Summary

Current main fixes the reported heartbeat timer overflow at the scheduler layer: oversized heartbeat delays are clamped to Node's safe setTimeout ceiling, logged once, and re-armed without running the heartbeat until the real due time. This shipped in v2026.4.24 via the changelog entry for #71478/#71414, superseding the older validation-only PR #61638 for the observable failure in #61546.
