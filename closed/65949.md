## Summary

Current `main` rebuilds the effective tool surface for each embedded run, reapplies the active tool allowlist to the live session, and `/new` or `/reset` now force a fresh underlying CLI binding. That covers the reported stale Discord-thread/browser-capability mismatch.

## Evidence
