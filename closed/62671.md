## Summary

Current `main` contains explicit iMessage outbound sanitization, reflected-message drop logic, loop-rate suppression, direct-chat `NO_REPLY` rewrite behavior, and the newer active-delivery-claim fix for reconnect/startup duplicate sends. Together those paths cover the stale replies, internal/status payload leakage, and `NO_REPLY` leakage described in #62671.

## Evidence

- **Outbound iMessage replies are sanitized before send:** `deliverReplies` runs every outbound iMessage payload through `sanitizeOutboundText(...)` before chunking and `sendMessageIMessage(...)`, so assistant-internal scaffolding is stripped on the direct iMessage delivery path.
