## Summary

Current `main` already passes cron `delivery.accountId` through the isolated-cron delivery path, gives it higher precedence than session/binding-derived accounts, and covers both override cases with regression tests. I could not tie that implementation to a released tag yet, so this looks fixed on `main` but not clearly shipped in a release.
