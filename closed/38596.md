## Summary

Keep #38596 open. Current main still has generic channel health-monitor restart/cooldown/hourly-cap behavior, but no monitor-level repeated-restart escalation, Discord clean-restart/fresh-IDENTIFY handoff across health-monitor restarts, or reconnect catch-up behavior that would resolve the reported post-connection zombie/ingest-lag pattern. The discussion also includes current-version production evidence and an open unmerged PR (#40413) targeting this issue.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #38596 open until a maintainer lands or explicitly rejects a focused Discord recovery design. The likely path is to evaluate PR #40413 or a narrower replacement that adds monitor-level repeated-restart escalation, exposes a Discord clean-restart/fresh-IDENTIFY handoff across the channel boundary, resets escalation after a stable period, and adds regression tests for health-monitor escalation plus Discord lifecycle recovery. Treat related ingest-lag reports such as #71546 as supporting evidence, not as a replacement for this monitor-escalation issue.
