## Summary

Current OpenClaw already avoids the reported long Moonshot/Kimi tool-call IDs: the Moonshot provider preserves native `functions.<name>:<index>` IDs instead of strict-rewriting them, and the generic strict sanitizer still clamps rewritten IDs to 40 characters on outbound replay/tool-continuation requests.

## Evidence
