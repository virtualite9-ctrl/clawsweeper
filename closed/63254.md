## Summary

Current `main` already contains dedicated fixes for the restart/recovery redelivery paths described here: persistent Matrix inbound event dedupe across restarts, cron startup catch-up guards that skip unsafe replay, and heartbeat wake coalescing/duplicate suppression for same-session main-lane delivery.

## Evidence

- **Matrix restart dedupe is persistent and wired into inbound handling:** The Matrix monitor now creates a persistent inbound dedupe store keyed by `roomId|eventId`, claims before handling, commits after successful handling, and releases on failure/abort. That directly targets the reporter's restart/recovery-bound duplicate `event_id` symptom.
