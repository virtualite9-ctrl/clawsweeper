## Summary

Current main addresses the reported Telegram silent-completion failure with delivery and lifecycle backstops: generated block replies are flushed before post-prompt compaction waits, lost compaction retry resolution has an aggregate timeout, dispatcher/typing cleanup runs in finally paths, and Telegram sends a fallback when dispatch ends with no visible delivery.
