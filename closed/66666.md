## Summary

Current main already has the requested capability split across existing plugin hooks: `llm_output` exposes generated assistant text after model completion, `message_sending` can rewrite or cancel outbound text before delivery, and `reply_dispatch` can take over the final reply pipeline. A new `before_delivery` hook would duplicate existing surfaces.

## Evidence

## Risks / Open Questions

- The gap looks like discoverability: the needed behavior is split across `llm_output`, `message_sending`, and `reply_dispatch`, so plugin authors may miss it if they only look at `before_agent_reply`.
