## Summary

Current `main` already routes spawned subagent-envelope sessions through the normal tool-policy pipeline, applies `tools.subagents.tools` there, supports additive `alsoAllow`, and only hard-denies a narrow set of session/control tools. In that implementation, `browser`, `web_search`, and `web_fetch` are not subagent-denied, and regression tests cover ACP children spawned under a subagent envelope.

## Evidence
