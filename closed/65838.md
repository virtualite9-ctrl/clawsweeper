## Summary

Current `main` already provides supported ways to disable Bonjour/mDNS advertising: `discovery.mdns.mode: "off"`, `OPENCLAW_DISABLE_BONJOUR=1`, or disabling the bundled `bonjour` plugin. The implementation, docs, and regression tests are present on `main`, but I could not tie the change to a released tag yet.

## Evidence
