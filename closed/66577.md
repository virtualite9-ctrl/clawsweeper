## Summary

The requested hard-truncation/sliding-window behavior is not part of the built-in legacy compaction modes on current `main`, but OpenClaw already treats alternative context-management policies as plugin/context-engine territory. This item is better closed as plugin-scope rather than adding another core compaction mode.

## Evidence
