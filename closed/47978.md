## Summary

Current main no longer matches the reported Telegram-validation fall-through. Cron delivery preserves the explicit delivery.channel, isolated cron passes that channel into delivery target resolution, and WebChat is handled as an internal-only/non-outbound target rather than being routed through Telegram validation.
