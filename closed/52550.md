## Summary

Current `main` already implements configurable rate-limit failover behavior for cross-provider/local fallback. The specific control is `auth.cooldowns.rateLimitedProfileRotations`; setting it to `0` skips same-provider auth-profile rotations and falls through immediately to the next configured fallback model.
